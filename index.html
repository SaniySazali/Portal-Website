About Majlis Sukan Negara Malaysia

Majlis Sukan Negara Malaysia (MSN) is dedicated to nurturing elite athletes and enhancing Malaysia’s sporting excellence through world-class training programmes, modern facilities, and comprehensive athlete development initiatives. MSN plays a vital role in preparing national athletes to compete successfully at regional and international levels.

Vision

To become a world-class sporting nation.

Mission

Developing elite athletes through systematic training and support.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Teamwork and Professionalism.
